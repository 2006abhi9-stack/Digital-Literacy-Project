# Responsible Social Media Checklist

## ✅ The Do's
* **Audit Privacy:** Regularly check who can see your posts and photos.
* **Showcase Skills:** Share your projects, certifications, and academic achievements.
* **Network Proactively:** Connect with alumni and professionals in your field.
* **Think Before Posting:** Ask yourself, "Would I be okay with a recruiter seeing this?"
* **Maintain Civility:** Keep your tone respectful, even when you disagree with others.

## ❌ The Don'ts
* **Overshare:** Avoid posting real-time locations or sensitive personal data.
* **Badmouth Institutions:** Never post negative comments about your university, faculty, or past employers.
* **Post Without Checking:** Avoid sharing unverified news or inflammatory content.
* **Ignore the "Paper Trail":** Don't forget that "deleted" posts can often still be found via screenshots or archives.
* **Prioritize Likes over Reputation:** Don't post controversial content just for engagement if it compromises your professional image.
