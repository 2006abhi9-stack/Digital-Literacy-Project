Welcome to the official repository for my Digital Literacy and Professional Development projects. As a Student Digital Ambassador, my goal is to bridge the gap between technical skill and responsible digital citizenship. This repository serves as a portfolio of work and a resource guide for CSE & AIML students.

🚀 Repository Overview

This project is divided into five core pillars of digital competency:

📽️ [Task 1] 
Digital Literacy Fundamentals
Visual resources and slides designed to educate students on the core tenets of digital literacy, focusing on information evaluation and digital citizenship.

🌐 [Task 2] 
Professional Digital Identity
Showcasing the "Digital Trifecta" for modern developers. This folder contains my professional architecture on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle, demonstrating how to maintain a consistent professional brand.

🐍 [Task 3]
Technical Foundations & Data Logic
Python Problem Solving: Solved challenges from HackerRank demonstrating logic building.

Data Collection: 
A Google Form framework designed to analyze and assess the digital literacy levels of my peers.

📧 [Task 4]
Professional Communication & Etiquette
A toolkit for navigating academic and corporate environments:

Email Protocols:
Formal templates for extension requests and internship inquiries.

Social Media Checklist: A social-media-checklist.md guide to maintaining a "recruiter-ready" online presence.

🛡️ [Task 5]
Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention
As an ambassador, security is a priority. This folder includes:

Ransomware Case Study: A deep dive into modern cyber threats.

Stay Safe Online Guide: A checklist covering UPI safety and official Indian reporting channels (1930 / cybercrime.gov.in).
